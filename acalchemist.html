Alies&Counter


WEAK VS (ENEMIES)

  • Ancient apparition

  • AA ultimate doesn't let Alchemist use his mad Chemical Rage to regen HP quickly.

  • Chaos knight

  • The passive skill of Chaos Knight hits the weakest point of Alchemist – his armor – and he can easily evade the Concoction by using his ultimate or Manta Style, which is assembled by all self-respecting Chaos Knights.

  • Slardar

  • Given the already low base armor of Alchemist, Slardar terrifically breaks through it with his ultimate, under which the Alchemist just breaks down.

  • Lifestealer

  • Lifestealer's Feast is good against high health and low armor Alchemist. Rage allows Lifestealer to not be targetable by Unstable Concoction, possibly making Alchemist stun himself.

COMBO (FRIENDS)

  • Troll warlord

  • Alchemist becomes a completely unstoppable beast when Troll activates Battle Trance.

  • Warlock

  • Warlock farms refresher whereas Alchemist farms aghanim's scepter and gives it to Warlock. As a result, four golems destroy the throne in a midgame.

STRONG VS (VICTIMS)

  • Phoenix

  • Thanks to mad attack speed Alchemist leaves no chance to use Supernova successfully.

  • Undying

  • Alchemist easily farms the zombies getting additional gold for free. Thanks to mad attack speed, Alchemist will destroy the Tomb very quickly.
